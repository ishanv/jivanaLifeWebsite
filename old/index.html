About Us.

Jivana Organ Donation society is an organization founded by four transplant recipients who have gone through the mental and physical obstacles themselves. They want to use the second life they have received through organ donation to share their experiences and educate public on the importance of organ donation. They also to be the support for the South Asians and others who may be on the waiting list or the ones going through the post transplant recovery.

Our Misson.

Jivana Organ Donation Society's mission is to raise awareness in the South Asian community about the importance of registering as a organ donor. To educate people on the transplant procedure, eliminate cultural beliefs and myths in the South Asian community which also prevents many from putting their names on the organ donation registry and encourage donors to come forward.

Our Reason.

Because, the key requirement for a successful organ transplant is that both the donor and recipient share the same blood type. But blood types are not equally distributed among all ethnic groups. Most of the South Asian community shares the same blood type, which is the blood type B. The shortage of type B organs is because people of South Asian descent are a lot less likely to register as organ donors.
